In my Ancient Hebrew Alphabet Chart I identify the different meanings of each Hebrew letter. How are these meanings determined? Each Hebrew letter was originally written as a picture, a pictograph. As an example, in the modern Hebrew alphabet the letter peh is written as פ, but in the ancient pictographic script this letter was a picture of a mouth.
